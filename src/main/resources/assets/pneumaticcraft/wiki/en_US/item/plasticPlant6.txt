The plant this seed belongs to is part of PneumaticCraft's [link{pneumaticcraft:menu/worldgen}]worldgen [link{}]. Generic information about all the plastic plants can be found by clicking on the link. The following is a summary of the behaviour of this particular plant:

[prefix{l}]Color: [prefix{}]
Cyan

[prefix{l}]Soil: [prefix{}]
Dirt, Grass and Farmland

[prefix{l}]How to acquire in survival:[prefix{}]
Lightning Plants are spawned on top of grass in the Overworld. Apart from that, any lightning strike has a potential to spawn a Lightning Plant at the location of impact.

[prefix{l}]full-grown behaviour:[prefix{}]
When it rains, the Lightning Plant will cause a lightning strike to a maximum of 15 blocks away of the plant.
Top tip: Combined with the [link{block/electrostaticCompressor}]Electrostatic Compressor[link{}] this is a good way of creating compressed air.

[prefix{l}]propagation theory:[prefix{}]
Causing lightning strikes which have a potential to grow new Lightning Plants.
