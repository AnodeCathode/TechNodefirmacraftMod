[prefix{l}]How to acquire in survival:[prefix{}]
Rain Plants are spawned on top of grass in the Overworld.

[prefix{l}]full-grown behaviour:[prefix{}]
When it rains, the Rain Plant will influence clouds. They will drop a Rain Plant Seed which eventually will make it's way to the ground.
